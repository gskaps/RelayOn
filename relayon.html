HOW IT WORKS

Grief doesn't end after
the first week.

RelayOn helps the people who care coordinate care that lasts — across weeks, across months.

01
Create a page for the family
Set up a simple, private page in minutes. Add the family's name, a note about what they need, and which weeks to cover.
02
Invite your people to contribute
Share a link with friends, family, or community. Anyone can claim a date, choose a category — meals, flowers, cleaning, errands — and schedule it out.
03
Care arrives, week after week
The family gets a gentle view of what's coming. No surprises, no gaps, no five lasagnas on the same Tuesday.
FEATURES

Built for the long haul of grief.

Everything a community needs to show up well — and keep showing up.

📅
Multi-month scheduling
Spread care across weeks and months — not just the first wave. Grief lingers, and so can your support.
🏪
Local vendor connections
Connect with local bakeries, florists, cleaning services, and meal delivery so contributors can send with just a few clicks.
🎛️
Fully customizable
Families can set preferences, dietary needs, and "no-thank-you" items. Contributors always know exactly what helps.
🔒
Private & gentle
No social feed, no likes, no noise. Just a quiet, coordinated place for real care — visible only to those invited.
📬
Gentle reminders
Contributors get a soft nudge before their date arrives — so nothing gets forgotten, and no one feels awkward following up.
🤝
One coordinator, many helpers
A single person can set it all up — then step back and let the community fill it in at their own pace.
